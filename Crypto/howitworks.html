How Gold NFT Works

Understand the seamless process that bridges the timeless value of physical gold with the innovative power of blockchain technology.

1

Physical Gold Acquisition & Auditing

We acquire high-purity, investment-grade physical gold from reputable suppliers. Each bar undergoes rigorous auditing by independent third parties to verify its weight, purity, and ethical sourcing. This ensures that every gram of gold backing our NFTs meets the highest industry standards.

2

Tokenization (Minting)

Once verified, the physical gold is tokenized into Gold NFTs on a secure blockchain. Each NFT is a unique digital representation, with its metadata immutably linked to a specific amount of physical gold. This process, known as minting, creates a digital certificate of ownership.

3

Secure Vault Storage

The physical gold backing your NFTs is stored in highly secure, insured, and independently audited vaults. This eliminates the need for you to worry about physical storage, insurance, or transportation, providing peace of mind and convenience.

4

Digital Ownership & Trading

Once minted, your Gold NFT resides in your digital wallet. You can then easily buy, sell, or trade your fractional gold ownership on our secure marketplace, just like any other digital asset, with instant settlement and global access.

5

Physical Redemption

At any time, if you hold a sufficient quantity of Gold NFTs, you have the option to redeem them for the corresponding physical gold. Our team will facilitate the secure delivery of your gold from the vault to your specified location, subject to our redemption policy.

Gold NFT simplifies gold ownership, making it more accessible, secure, and liquid than ever before.
